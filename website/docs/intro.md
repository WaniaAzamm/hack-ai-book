# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook, an academic resource designed for advanced robotics education.

## About This Textbook

This textbook covers the fundamental concepts, mathematical foundations, and implementation strategies for Physical AI systems, with particular focus on humanoid robotics applications. Each module builds upon previous knowledge to provide a comprehensive understanding of how artificial intelligence can be embedded in physical systems.

## Modules

The textbook is organized into multiple modules, each containing several chapters:

- **Module 1**: Foundations of Physical AI
  - Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI
  - Chapter 2: Mathematical Foundations of Physical AI
  - Chapter 3: Implementation Strategies for Physical AI Systems

## Academic Standards

This textbook adheres to rigorous academic standards:
- All content is verified from primary academic sources
- Minimum 15 sources per major section
- Peer-reviewed content with proper APA citations
- Flesch-Kincaid grade level 10-12 for accessibility
- Target 5,000-7,000 words per major section
